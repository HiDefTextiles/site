# About

**HiDef textiles** connects smart knitting, research software, and public outreach. We bridge 
**sustainability**, **technology**, and **Icelandic cultural heritage** through interdisciplinary collaboration.

## Goals
- Develop user-friendly methods and tools for smart knitting
- Share process and results with the public and the education community
- Support practical innovation and education
